Build-A-Bod
Build-A-Bod is a social network iOS app that promotes fitness.

It incorporates iOS, Firebase, and TensorFlow. Users can message friends and challenge one another to perform an exercise such as push ups. Proper form will be verified using our Machine Learning model and points will be accordingly awarded based on how well the exercise was performed.

Developed at HackDavis 2019.
